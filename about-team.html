Our Story

Our journey began with a mission: to protect your data
with blockchain, encryption, and global innovation. This
is our story—building trust in a digital world.

StorX Network's journey began in 2021, during the peak of the global data privacy crisis. Major cloud storage providers were repeatedly falling victim to cyberattacks and users were losing faith in centralized systems. Mr. Handy Barot, a seasoned IT professional embarked on a journey that would change the way the world thought about data storage. With over 20 years of experience in designing, setup and management of IT infrastructure. Handy saw an opportunity to leverage blockchain technology to create a decentralized storage solution that would give users complete control over their data.

The Core Principles: Privacy, Security, and Decentralization From the very beginning, Handy was adamant about three core principles that would define StorX Network:

  • Absolute Privacy: Users would have full control over their data. No one, not even StorX Network, could access or view their files without their permission.
  • Unbreakable Security: By leveraging blockchain’s immutable ledger and advanced encryption, StorX Network would ensure that data was protected from hackers and unauthorized access.
  • True Decentralization: Unlike traditional cloud storage providers, StorX would operate on a peer-to-peer network, eliminating single points of failure and reducing the risk of data breaches.
