Bug Bounty Program for StorX

StorX is happy to release the Bug Bounty Program for testing StorX Platform.

StorX welcomes all to test StorX platform for any errors, bugs and grab the bounty. We also offer bounty for to enhance it.

Rules

  • Please provide detailed reports with reproducible steps. If the report is not detailed enough to reproduce the issue, the issue will not be eligible for a reward.
  • Submit one vulnerability per report, unless you need to chain vulnerabilities to provide impact.
  • When duplicates occur, we only award the first report that was received.
  • Multiple vulnerabilities caused by one underlying issue will be awarded one bounty.
  • Phishing, Vishing, Smishing is prohibited.
  • Make a good faith effort to avoid privacy violations, destruction of data, and interruption or degradation of our service. Only interact with accounts you own or with explicit permission of the account holder.

Out of scope Vulnerabilities

The following issues are considered out of scope:

  • Content spoofing and text injection issues without showing an attack vector/without being able to modify HTML/CSS.
  • Clickjacking on pages with no sensitive actions.
  • Attacks requiring MITM or physical access to a user's device.
  • Previously known vulnerable libraries without a working Proof of Concept.
  • Comma Separated Values (CSV) injection without demonstrating a vulnerability.
  • Missing best practices in SSL/TLS configuration.
  • Any activity that could lead to the disruption of our service (DoS).
  • Unauthenticated/logout/login CSRF.
  • When reporting vulnerabilities, please consider attack scenario/exploitability, and security impact of the bug.

Rewards

Rewards are based on the Common Vulnerability Scoring Standard.

Up to 100 USD worth StorX

Special Projects Bounty

Are you a software development company working with Government, Banks, or Fortune 500 institutions?

If you aare software development company working with Government, Central Banks, or Fortune 500 institutions? If you choose to build your solution on StorX Network & qualify for a special project then you can secure up to 200% reimbursement on your project fees in Tokens. i,.e. if you incur a cost of USD 25,000 for the project working with Government, Banks or Fortune 500 companies, then your bounty will be up to USD 50,000 equivalent StorX as per market rate.

  • The project should be directed for Banks, Governments or Fortune 500 companies.

  • The project should preferably be open source.

  • Share estimate budget for the project with StorX Team and get pre-approval on your Project and Project Cost.
  • On successful completion and deployment You will get reimbursement for up to 200% of the fees incurred in your wallet.

If you are willing to pay for technical integration fee for an exchange, then you will get up to 140% of the tech integration fees in StorX, i.e. if you incur a cost of USD 50,000 for technical integration with a crypto exchange, then your bounty will be up to USD 70,000 equivalent StorX as per market rate.

  • Include support@Storx.tech in your communication with the exchange technical integration team.
