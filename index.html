StorX Decentralized cloud storage solution

StorX offers decentralized cloud storage platform that connects users who need file storage with hosts worldwide offering underutilized storage capacity. Riding on Blockchain technology data is completely secured, No intermediaries ensures economics for users and hosts.

Our Principles

100% Decentralization.

Privacy and Security

StorX encrypts and distributes the files across decentralized network. We believe that privacy is a fundamental human right. Everyone has the right of a secure communication. It's the main motivation behind all our products.

Blockchain technology ensures that your data is stored with 100% reliability.

Affordability

Due to its undermining distributed architecture, StorX is able to offer storage at almost 80 - 90% less compared to enterprise cloud storage peers. Storing 1TB of files on StorX costs about $ 2 - 4 per month, compared with $23 on Amazon S3.

Open source

Everything we do is 100% open source. We do not do open core or proprietary extensions. We use and support open standards as much as possible. We don't lock people in to proprietary formats. You can import and export everything.

Community Development

StorX is able to offer new improved features due to ongoing support from its community. Our community members empower our platform and contribute.
