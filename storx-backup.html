Backup & Archival Services on StorX

StorX Network was established with the vision to
provide privacy focused, highly secured, high
performance backup on decentralise cloud for
Individual, SMBs, SMEs, and large enterprises.

In today's digital world, data is your most valuable asset. Whether it's personal files, important documents, or business-critical data, losing it can have catastrophic consequences. Traditional backup solutions often rely on centralized cloud storage, where all your data is stored in one location, making it vulnerable to breaches, outages, or even data loss.

With StorX Network, you can take complete control of your data and back it up more securely than ever before. By distributing your files across multiple independent nodes, StorX Network offers enhanced security, greater reliability, and unmatched control over your backup data.

Switch to StorX Network today and ensure that your backup data is always safe, accessible, and under your control

Features

End-to-End Encryption

Your backup data is encrypted before it leaves your device and remains encrypted throughout its journey and storage. This ensures that only you and those you authorize can access it, providing the highest level of security.

Data Fragmentation

Your data is securely split and distributed across multiple independent nodes, removing the risks associated with a single point of failure. This ensures your backup remains accessible, even if one or more nodes experience issues.

Low Latency & High Availability

Data is backed up across multiple locations, ensuring minimal latency and continuous availability. Even in the event of server outages, your backup data is still accessible.

Redundant Storage

Multiple copies of your data are stored on different nodes, ensuring availability even if some nodes go offline.

Blockchain Integration

The location of your data fragments is recorded on a blockchain, creating an immutable record of your backups.

Automated Backups

Set up scheduled backups to ensure your data is always protected without manual intervention.
